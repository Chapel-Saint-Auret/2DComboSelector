## Elution Domain Utility

Normalized utility derived from Elution Domain (%) and used in the final score calculation.

It is calculated as:

	-**U_D = D / 100**

where **D** is the Elution Domain expressed as a percentage of the theoretical maximum 2D elution-composition space.

Values range from **0** to **1**, with higher values indicating a larger covered elution-composition domain.
