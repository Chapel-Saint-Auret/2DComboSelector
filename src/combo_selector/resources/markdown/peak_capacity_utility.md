## Peak Capacity Utility

Normalized utility derived from the Hypothetical 2D Peak Capacity and used in the final score calculation.

It is calculated as:

	-**U_P = (P - P_min) / (P_max - P_min)**
where **P** is the Hypothetical 2D Peak Capacity, and **P_min** and **P_max** are the minimum and maximum values observed across all tested combinations.

Values range from **0** to **1**, with higher values indicating greater theoretical separation potential.
