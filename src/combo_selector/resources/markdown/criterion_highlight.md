## Criterion Highlight

Indicates whether a combination belongs to the Top 1%, Top 5%, or Top 10% for one or more ranking criteria.

These highlights are based on criterion-specific ranks and are used to identify combinations that perform particularly well 

on individual criteria, even if they are not ranked first overall.

Legend : Orthogonality (O), Peak capacity (n2D), Elution Domain (Δφ2D)
