## Orthogonality Rank

Normalized utility derived from the Orthogonality Rank and used in the final score calculation.

It is calculated as:

	-**U_O = 1 - (R_O - 1) / (N - 1)**
where **R_O** is the Orthogonality Rank and N is the total number of combinations.

Values range from **0** to **1**, with higher values indicating better orthogonality.
